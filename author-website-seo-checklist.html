FAQs

Questions authors ask before building a book website

How do I market my book?

Market your book by making it easy to find, easy to understand, easy to trust and easy to buy. Start with a clear author website, one strong book page, buy links, reader proof, social content, email capture and public information that Google and AI chats can read.

How can I market my book with no audience?

Start with a small system. Build a book landing page, explain the book clearly, collect emails, post useful or entertaining content, appear on small podcasts or blogs, and send every bit of attention back to one clear page.

How do I market my novel online?

To market a novel online, make the genre, hook, characters, themes, reader promise and buy links obvious. Then create social content around scenes, tropes, quotes, worldbuilding, behind-the-scenes notes and the emotional reason readers should care.

What is the best way to advertise a novel?

The best way to advertise a novel is to send attention to a page that can convert. Ads work better when the landing page shows the cover, hook, genre, reviews, sample, author bio and clear buy links.

Do authors need a website?

Authors can promote without a website, but a website gives readers, Google, journalists, reviewers and AI chats one reliable place to understand the author, book, genre, proof and next step.
