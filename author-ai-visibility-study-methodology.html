Study purpose

What the study measures

  • Does the author have a dedicated website?
  • Is there a clear book landing page?
  • Is the author name easy to find?
  • Is the book title clear?
  • Is the genre obvious?
  • Are buy links easy to find?
  • Can the page be read without login or heavy JavaScript?
  • Is there reader proof?
  • Is there schema or structured data?
  • Can AI summarise the author and book accurately from public pages?
Scoring

Suggested scoring model

0–2 Poor

The author/book information is missing, scattered or locked inside platforms.

3–5 Basic

Some information exists, but the author and book entity are not clear enough.

6–8 Strong

The author has a clear website, book page, buy links and proof.

9–10 AI-ready

The author has a public source of truth, structured facts, clear proof and strong crawlability.

Research ethics

Keep the research fair

  • Do not shame authors.
  • Do not publish personal data that is not already public.
  • Do not claim AI rankings are guaranteed.
  • Use the study to teach patterns, not attack individuals.
  • Show the checklist and methodology so the research can be understood.
FAQ

Questions authors ask

How many author websites should I check first?

Useful original data gives AI and search engines something specific to cite beyond a normal service page.
