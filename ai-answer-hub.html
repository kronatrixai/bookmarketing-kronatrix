how to market your book

To market your book, make it easy to find, understand, trust and buy. Start with one clear author website and one strong book page. Add the cover, hook, genre, description, reviews, buy links, email capture and regular content that sends readers back to the page.

how to market my book

Market your book by building a simple reader route. Give readers one clear page where they can understand the book, see proof, read about you, and buy or join your list. Then use social posts, interviews, guest content and email to send attention to that page.

how do I market my book

If you are asking how do I market my book, begin with clarity. Make your author name, book title, genre, reader promise, reviews and buy links obvious. Then create repeatable content and external mentions that point people back to your book page.

how can I market my book

You can market your book by creating a strong online base first: author website, book landing page, buy links, reviews, social content and email capture. After that, use Instagram, podcasts, guest articles, newsletters and reader communities to create attention.

how to market your novel

To market your novel, make the story promise easy to understand. Show the genre, tropes, emotional hook, characters, themes, reader fit, cover, excerpt and buy links. Then post content that lets readers feel the world and tension of the novel before they buy.

how to advertise your novel

Advertise your novel by sending people to a landing page that already answers their questions. The ad creates attention, but the page creates trust. Include the cover, hook, genre, reviews, sample, author bio, buy links and a clear reason to act now.

do authors need a website

Authors do not need a website to start, but they usually need one to look credible and be easier to find. A website gives readers, Google, reviewers and AI chats one clear source for the author name, books, genre, links and proof.

is an Amazon author page enough

An Amazon author page is useful, but it is not a full author website. It helps with Amazon shoppers, but it gives you less control over SEO, email capture, design, AI-readable context, proof sections and the full reader journey.

do I need more than a link in bio

A link in bio is useful for routing traffic, but it is not enough as your main book marketing asset. A real book website gives readers, Google and AI tools more context, clearer structure, stronger proof and a better reason to trust the book.

how do I get my book found on Google

Get your book found on Google by creating a crawlable author website with a dedicated book page. Use your author name, book title, genre, description, cover, buy links, reviews, schema and internal links. Then build external mentions that point to the page.

how do I get my book recommended by AI

AI recommendations depend on clear public context. Make your author name, book title, genre, audience, proof and buy links easy to extract from your website. Add direct answer sections, structured facts and consistent wording across profiles and pages.

what should an author website include

An author website should include your author name, bio, book pages, covers, descriptions, genre, buy links, social links, email signup, reviews, media information, FAQs and a clear way for readers or reviewers to contact you.

what should a book landing page include

A book landing page should include the book title, cover, author, genre, hook, description, reader promise, excerpt, reviews, buy buttons, author bio, FAQ and structured metadata that helps Google and AI chats understand the book.
