Short answer

How authors should use Instagram

Hook writers and readers

Use prompts, character questions, quote reels and behind-the-scenes posts.

Create comments

Ask questions writers want to answer, not generic engagement bait.

Send traffic somewhere

Link attention back to a book page, author website or free sample.

Capture interest

Use email capture or a clear buy/read path so attention does not disappear.

Content ideas

Post ideas that can lead back to the site

  • Write the first line of a book that would make someone stop scrolling.
  • Your villain sends a text at 2:13am. What does it say?
  • Describe your book badly in one sentence.
  • Write the sentence that turns a best friend into an enemy.
  • If your book was a warning label, what would it say?
  • Show the book page and ask if the genre is clear.
