# 30-Day Post-Launch Plan

## Day 1
- Upload site to GitHub.
- Confirm custom domain and HTTPS.
- Test all Formspree forms.
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.

## Days 2–7
- Request indexing for the 10 most important pages.
- Add footer backlinks from every proof website.
- Publish one LinkedIn post about the Free Author AI Visibility Check.
- Publish one Medium or Substack article linking to the source-of-truth page.

## Days 8–14
- Add real screenshots to the screenshot gallery.
- Start the Author AI Visibility Study with 20 author websites.
- DM/email 20 authors with a free check offer.

## Days 15–30
- Publish the first study findings.
- Add real FAQs from author replies.
- Improve pages that get Search Console impressions.
- Add testimonials only after real permission.
