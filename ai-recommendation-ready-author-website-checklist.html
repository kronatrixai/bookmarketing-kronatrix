Checklist

Is your author website ready to be recommended by AI?

This checklist is built for authors who want their book to be easier for AI search tools to understand, summarise and recommend.

Entity facts

  • Author name is consistent
  • Book title is clear
  • Genre is obvious
  • Author bio exists
  • Social links match

Book facts

  • Cover image is visible
  • Description is specific
  • Reader promise is clear
  • Buy links are easy to find
  • Excerpt or sample exists

AI/search facts

  • Page is crawlable
  • Schema exists
  • FAQ answers exist
  • Sitemap includes the page
  • External mentions support it
