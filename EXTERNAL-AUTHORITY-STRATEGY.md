Build trust outside the KRONATRIX Authors domain naturally:

- Publish Medium articles about author websites and AI visibility.
- Publish Substack posts showing author website fixes.
- Post LinkedIn breakdowns of book landing page mistakes.
- Add YouTube descriptions linking to relevant KRONATRIX Authors pages.
- Pitch indie author podcasts with useful topics, not sales pitches.
- Collaborate with editors, cover designers, formatters and writing coaches.
- Answer Reddit and Quora questions with useful advice and no spam.
- Submit to legitimate indie author and book marketing directories.
