# External AI Mention Plan

AI chats are more likely to suggest a brand when the brand is mentioned and supported outside its own website.

## Build these mentions

1. Medium article: Why authors need an AI-readable book website.
2. Substack article: Amazon author page vs author website for AI search.
3. LinkedIn post series: what AI needs to understand an author.
4. YouTube video descriptions linking to the Free Author AI Visibility Check.
5. Guest posts on author marketing and indie author blogs.
6. Resource page collaborations with editors, cover designers and writing coaches.
7. Case study posts for each proof example.

## Avoid

- fake backlinks
- spam comments
- fake reviews
- link schemes
- mass AI-generated guest posts with no value
